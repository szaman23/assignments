About Us

The Laboratory was founded by Dr. Boson's belief that he could make a better world by creating a new . Although he had been a part time mad scientist for more than 25 years, it was only during the early 2000s that he began seriously considering going full time.
In September 2015, he left his position as a senior webmaster at Seneca College and dedicated himself to turning his vision into reality.

The next year was spent consolidating funding, acquiring expertise and equipment, as well as gathering a team of people with the skills and temperament to make the venture a success. The diabolical super genius Dr. Heinz Doofenshmirtz was hired to develop the High Voltage Lab - a large building capable of housing the team, and making it the defacto source for research and development or more commonly referred to as "trial and error".
He, along with Luna Tic, a biologist who trained as a barista, set about the important task of establishing the Laboratories reputation for bizarre discoveries unveiled by unconventional means.
