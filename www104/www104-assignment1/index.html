Mad Scientists

Mad Scientist Laboratories is a collection of some of Earth's most passionate scientists.

Our crazed pursuit of knowledge is driven by our desire to make the world a better place.

We believe in changing the world, and we believe the best way to do that is in lab coats and goggles! The Laboratory is home to the worlds most eccentric experts in their fields.

Our Staff

Mad Scientist Laboratories began operations in September 2014.

Located at 1234 Labrat Valley Drive in Toronto's tech district, we have established ourselves as Canada's first and foremost super genius team.

Its founder, Dr. Higgs Boson, has since been instrumental in collecting experts from around the country including Professor Geology Rocks, Walter Bishop, Dr. Evil, Sue from Accounting, and the mysterious Dr. Weird.

Our Lab

The Laboratory was founded by Dr. Boson's belief that he could make a better world by creating a new one.

Although he had been a part time mad scientist for more than 25 years, it was only during the early 2000s that he began seriously considering going full time.

In September 2015, he left his position as a senior webmaster at Seneca College and dedicated himself to turning his vision into reality.
